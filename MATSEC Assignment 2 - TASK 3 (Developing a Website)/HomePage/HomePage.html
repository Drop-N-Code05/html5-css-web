St Thomas More Secondary School, Zejtun, is a co-educational school catering for students ages 11 to 16 years of age. The catchment area of the school is primarily Zejtun and Marsascala. The school population is currently 680 students and 160 members of staff. The school offers its student population programmes leading to the SEC ordinary level certificates for high achievers. There are many subjects studied at this school. One of them is Information Technology. Information technology is the use of computers to store, retrieve, transmit, and manipulate data or information. IT is typically used within the context of business operations as opposed to personal or entertainment technologies. IT is considered to be a subset of information and communications technology.
